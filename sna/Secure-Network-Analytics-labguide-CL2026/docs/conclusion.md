# Conclusion

In this lab, you used **Splunk Cloud** and **Cisco Secure Network Analytics** to gain visibility into **asset utilization**.

Together, these steps show how **NetFlow telemetry** collected by SNA can be **consumed in Splunk** for centralized analysis and storytelling about **who is busy**, **how they are accessed**, and **which assets appear idle** in a chosen window.

**Key takeaways**

- **Cisco SNA** provides flow visibility by collecting and analyzing NetFlow (and related telemetry) from monitored devices.
- **Splunk Cloud** supports flexible searches, dashboards, and drill-downs on that telemetry.
- **Asset utilization** benefits from both **host-level** views (which devices move the most bytes or flows) and **flow-level** views (who talked to whom, how, and for how long).
- Combining **SNA** and **Splunk** gives a practical base for **capacity**, **security**, and always validating “idle” labels against process and longer time ranges.
