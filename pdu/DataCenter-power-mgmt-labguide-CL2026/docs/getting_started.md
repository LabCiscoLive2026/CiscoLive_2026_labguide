## Access Requirements

### Connectivity and Hardware

- **Network Access:** AI Era Power management dashboards are hosted on Splunk Cloud instance. Please ensure you have a stable internet connection for the duration of the session.
- **Recommended Hardware:** A laptop or tablet is recommended to ensure full compatibility and optimal interaction with the dashboard interfaces.
- **Browser:** Chrome preferred
